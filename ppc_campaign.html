PPC Campaign Management

Sales up! TACOS down! That's the rule we go by

There's no denying the importance of having a successful business.Amazon PPC campaign runs parallel, in terms of achieving the end result that is: More sales, to other marketing strategies such as ManyChat, ERP (Early Reviewer Program), Deals and Coupons on Social Media Marketing or product website. Hence Enetwa gives utmost priority into finelining Amazon’s PPC campaigns for your brand.

Our team of expert campaign managers establish a specialised plan of action for every product that we launch, in order to boost sales for the listing from day one as well as keeping the overall spend in mind.

There is no uniform strategy for Amazon PPC, plan is to always create specialised strategy based on the niche, competition, price and the overall utility of the product. Our strategies are usually aggressive in the starting, that is because we build prominence in front of Amazon by bidding relatively higher for keywords we think will us the most in the future, and once we see Amazon’s algorithm has started building our prominence for those keywords, we start diversifying into different other keywords that will give us lower conversion for Lower CPC.This is just one strategy we have discussed, our team of experts launch hundreds of such strategies on daily basis to test the best outcomes.
